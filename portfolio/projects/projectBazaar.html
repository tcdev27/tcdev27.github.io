Project Bazaar

As part of my degree at Edge Hill I am undertaking a Final Year Project. That Project is Project Bazaar. By the end of this "Project" won't look like a real word !

It involves the design and development of a web application which provides 3 key services:

  • A Blog that Guides Students through certain areas of their own projects.
  • A Place for Tutors to advertise their own project ideas where students can then pick one and register their interest to undertake them.
  • A Collection of Samples of work for students to view and see how they should approach particular aspects of their project documentation.
